Which zone should be the default?

This addon uses the concept of "zones" to indicate how strictly it filters images.






Show an on/off switch on the main menu?

Add a switch to turn filtering on and off quickly while browsing. Note: already-blocked images will only reappear when the cache is cleared - hit shift+refresh on the page to reload it fully. This also controls the Reveal Blocked Image menu visibility.



Video blocking?

Video blocking requires quite a bit of processing, and the accuracy is currently lower due to technical limitations.
Additionally, not all video sites are supported because video data is delivered to the browser in many ways.



Quick Scan will scan only a small selection of frames, but provides a lighter, balanced experience and is the default behavior.

Silent mode?

Instead of showing a blocked icon, instead choose a placeholder image.
